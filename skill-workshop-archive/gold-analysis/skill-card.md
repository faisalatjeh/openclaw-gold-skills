## Description: <br>
Gold Analysis helps agents produce structured gold market analysis by combining price, technical, macroeconomic, sentiment, and geopolitical signals. <br>

This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Publisher: <br>
[FMouseBoy](https://clawhub.ai/user/FMouseBoy) <br>

### License/Terms of Use: <br>
MIT-0 <br>


## Use Case: <br>
Analysts, traders, and finance-focused agents use this skill to draft gold market outlooks, compare signals from multiple public sources, and produce informational trading guidance with risk notes. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Generated buy, sell, target, stop-loss, or position-size guidance may be mistaken for personalized investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat outputs as informational analysis only and confirm decisions with qualified financial advice and the user's own risk controls. <br>
Risk: Market prices, macroeconomic data, and news can become stale or conflict across sources. <br>
Mitigation: Verify live prices, calendar events, and news independently before relying on any generated analysis. <br>
