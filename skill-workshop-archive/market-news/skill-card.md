This skill is ready for commercial/non-commercial use. <br>

## Use Case: <br>
External users and market analysts use this skill to summarize financial news, interpret economic policy and company announcements, and review market movement across A-share, U.S., Hong Kong, and commodity futures markets. <br>

### Deployment Geography for Use: <br>
Global <br>

## Known Risks and Mitigations: <br>
Risk: Financial news summaries and policy or market interpretations may be incomplete, outdated, or subjective. <br>
Mitigation: Verify information against official filings, regulator notices, exchange data, and multiple reputable news sources before relying on it. <br>
Risk: Generated market commentary could be mistaken for investment advice. <br>
Mitigation: Treat the output as informational analysis only and apply independent professional judgment before making financial decisions. <br>
